What you need to get started

Creating the perfect cup of tea takes time, resources and patients, below are the REQUIRED ingredients and tools to make the perfect tea:

  • Kettle
  • Spoon
  • Teabag (Twinings if possible)
  • Milk
  • Boiling water
  • Sugar (optional)

Ingedients Order

The CORRECT order of these ingredients are as followed:

  1. Teabag
  2. Sugar (if chosen)
  3. Boiling water
  4. Milk

It is a cardinal sin to put milk in first!!!
